LeSean "Shady" McCoy - Pitt Panthers / Philadelphia Eagles

Bio

LeSean McCoy lit up Heinz Field in just two seasons at Pitt (2007–2008), rushing for nearly 2,900 yards and 35 touchdowns. He’s best remembered for powering Pitt’s stunning 13–9 upset over #2 West Virginia in the 2007 Backyard Brawl. Drafted by the Philadelphia Eagles, McCoy became their all-time leading rusher, earned six Pro Bowl selections, and won two Super Bowls before retiring as one of the most exciting backs of his era.
