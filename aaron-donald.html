Aaron Donald - Pitt Panthers / Los Angeles Rams

Bio

Aaron Donald dominated the trenches at Pitt from 2010–2013, becoming one of the most decorated defenders in school history. In his senior year, he swept the nation’s top defensive awards, including the Outland Trophy, Bednarik Award, Nagurski Trophy, and Lombardi Award, while earning consensus All-American honors. Drafted 13th overall by the Rams, he went on to become a 3-time NFL Defensive Player of the Year, 8-time Pro Bowler, and Super Bowl LVI champion.
