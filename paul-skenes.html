Paul Skenes - Pittsburgh Pirates

Bio

Paul Skenes, the top pick in the 2023 MLB Draft, wasted no time making an impact with the Pirates. Known for his triple-digit fastball and commanding presence on the mound, he quickly established himself as one of baseball’s brightest young stars. With a rare combination of power and poise, Skenes is viewed as the future ace capable of leading Pittsburgh back into contention.
