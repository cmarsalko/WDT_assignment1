Sidney Crosby - Pittsburgh Penguins

Bio

Sidney Crosby, the first overall pick in the 2005 NHL Draft, has been the face of the Pittsburgh Penguins for nearly two decades. Nicknamed “Sid the Kid,” he quickly lived up to the hype, leading the team to three Stanley Cup championships (2009, 2016, 2017). A two-time league MVP and widely regarded as one of the greatest players in hockey history, Crosby is known for his leadership, vision, and clutch performances on the biggest stages.
