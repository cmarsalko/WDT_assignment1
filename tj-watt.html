T.J. Watt - Pittsburgh Steelers

Bio

T.J. Watt has carved out his own legacy in Pittsburgh since being drafted in 2017. Known for his relentless motor and pass-rushing dominance, Watt tied the NFL’s single-season sack record with 22.5 sacks in 2021, earning NFL Defensive Player of the Year honors. A multiple-time Pro Bowler and team leader, he continues to anchor the Steelers’ defense and carry on the franchise’s tradition of great linebackers.
